What is LCNEM?

Crypto-currencies, of which transactions are managed in the blockchain, can be used with very reasonable fee compared to existing remittance systems, but they can't be used without price fluctuation risk.
LCNEM issue digital money of legal currencies, not crypto-currencies and manage transactions in the blockchain.
Collateral assets are deposited to govornment, so risk of issueance without collateralizing are suppressed.
We use NEM for blockchain.

To create friendly economy

Transaction fee is very reasonable in comparison to existing systems, so it is suited for remittance and payment.
In addition, it is also suited for present, tip, support, and so on.

LCNEM Wallet

This is a wallet of NEM blockchain which can be used with google account login.
Your private keys are linked with the google account, so managing private key which is required in existing wallets, is not needed.
